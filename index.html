About

Hackathons have been a regular annual event in the repertoire of technical events organized by our college since last 7 years. Imagine Hack is one such Hackathon. It firmly abides by the motto of our student society i.e. “Innovate, Invent and Inspire”. Imagine Hack was first organized in 2019 and turned out to be a huge success. The upcoming version of Imagine Hack is set to become the first-of-its-kind organized at our college. Our goal is to upstage the standard of hackathons in our city by creating an exclusive hacker experience, learning from some of the best internationally listed hackathons. We aim to bring corporate networking, placement opportunities, internship prospects, and incubation possibilities through the care of our sponsors and community partners..


Moreover, hackathons instill coding instinct into students, provide a kick start for beginners, establish a platform to showcase skills, and distill visionary concepts into actionable solutions, further inspiring us to organize Imagine Hack. With Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors from our college, immense resources are at our disposal and a campus with spacious auditorium, internet facilities, security and infrastructure in the heart of the city, a formidable experience is right at bay.

Mass tech gathering

Tech giants, media, acadmecians and students shall gather to connect and work towards creating products that benefit the society and potentially land into the market.

Startup catalyst

Similar to the product cycles of a startup company, hackathon teams shall quickly transition from the idea stage to the final product by one-on-one interaction with experts.

Talent hunt

Hackers get to showcase their skills before a barrage of Industry experts and a huge audience,which brings out their best version. Big corporations shall get first hand exposure to raw talent.

Win-Win scenario

We are looking at a fun filled and exciting event which involves networking between students, corporations, colleges and media which caters towards brand recognition, product reviews, potential employment opportunities and corporate partnerships

#Blockchain

This incredibly popular technology is an online distribution system that can store information without any central authority. This secure technology with the peer-to-peer nature of work has the potential to change the way the world has been working for centuries. Blockchain Technology isn’t the solution to every problem but the future of this world can be great if we let this technology grow. Any project in this domain would be greatly appreciated.

FAQ

  • What is a hackathon?+

    A hackathon can be best described as an “invention marathon”, or in simple words hackathons are about bringing your crazy ideas to reality. It’s an event where any tech-enthusiast, regardless of their individual field can come & participate to learn, build & share their creations over the course of a weekend, in a relaxed & productive environment. Hackathons are simply a venue for self-expression & creativity, where people come together & transform their thoughts into reality, through technology.

  • Can I start working on my hack before the event?+

    No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before Imagine Hack begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand.

  • Who all can participate?+

    This is a student hackathon and only students are allowed to participate. You’ll be required to verify at the event by showing your student ID card.

  • Do I need to have any specific qualifications for the hackathon?+

    If you love to code, you are more than welcome to participate in the hackathon. Though we may screen applications to maintain high standards..

  • I am a newbie, can I hack?+

    Ofcourse you can! We at Imagine Hack ensure that no one is left behind during the hacking, thanks to our mentors and volunteers who help us achieve the same. We make sure that you learn the best you can alongside having fun and experiencing what it feels to belong to a community.

  • How big a team can be?+

    The maximum team size is five and minimum being three.

  • What to bring to the hackathon?+

    Please bring a valid university ID (or any student ID if in high school), a computer (preferably a laptop), chargers, and any hardware you will use for your hack. Please bring your own extensions although we'll have them available. No firearms, weapons, alcohol, or illegal drugs are allowed on campus.

  • Does ImagineHack helps with travel reimbursement?+

    We surely fancy participents from across the country, however currently we do not help with any travel reimbursement.

  • How Does Judging works?+

    A panel of some of the biggest names in tech will evaluate hacks based on creativity, technical difficulty, design, and usefulness. The top 10 overall projects will demo what they have built-in front of all during our closing ceremonies. New judges will be announced on a rolling basis on Instagram and Facebook, so make sure to follow us!

  • Don't find my question listed here. What can I do?+

    You can always contact us at mss@mjcollege.ac.in or reach out to us on Instagram at @mssmjcet. We would be more than happy to help you.
